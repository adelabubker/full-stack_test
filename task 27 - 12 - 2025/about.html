Our Story

Founded in 2010, Auto Follow has been at the forefront of industrial automation innovation. We started with a simple mission: to make advanced automation accessible to businesses of all sizes. Today, we serve over 500 companies worldwide, helping them transform their operations through smart automation solutions.
